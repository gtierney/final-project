about

The Kangaroo Sanctuary was established by documentary star, Brolga. He stars in the documentary series, Kangaroo Dundee. In 2005 Brolga estabilished the first baby kangaroo rescue center in Alice Springs then built his own wildlife sanctuary in 2009. Donations are welcome to help Brolga complete constuction of the hospital to care for baby and adult kangaroos.
