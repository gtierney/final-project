about

When officials in Sochi began to plan to poison stray dogs in preparation for 2014 Winter Olympics, Vlada Provotorova and some friends found a piece of land outside of town and started Sochi's first ever all volunteer animal shelter. Since started Sochi Dogs has changed over 500 dogs lives. Despite the olympics and consistant adoptions being over the number of dogs continues to rise.

mission statement

Sochi Dogs mission is
1. To save as many dogs as possible and give them good homes.
2. To raise awareness.
3. To promote spay/neuter programs.
4. To encourage individuals to take responsibility for their dogs and to adopt stray dogs.
