about

Bear Creek Exotic Wildlife Sanctuary is a volunteer driven Private Foundation dedicated to the survival of rare and endangered species. They rescue and rehabilitate animals as well as provide care and a safe haven for abused, unwanted and injured exotic and zoo animals. Over 100 animals are housed at Bear Creek and emphasize the importance of educating children to understand and respect animals around us.
