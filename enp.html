about

Elephant Nature Park is a center based around the rescue and rehabilitation of elephants in Chiang Mai, Thailand. The park provides a natural environment for elephants and other animals including dogs, cats, buffaloes among many others. One of their many goals is to plant 25 acres of mountainside every year for 5 years in their effort to restore the surrounding rain forest.

mission statement

There mission statement is rooted in five principles.
1. To provide homes, welfare and the contribute to the development of the animals.
2. To bring ecological balance to the plants and animals through planting trees.
3. To maintain the cultural integrity of the local community through creating employment opportunities, and purchasing locally grown agricultural products.
4. To educate visitors on the predicament of the endangered local species.
5. To act independantly of any and all pressure groups and political movements that go against the wellbeing of the park and it's inhabitants.
