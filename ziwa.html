about

Ziwa Rhino Sanctuary was created from Rhino Fund Uganda (RFU). The ultimate goal of the RFU was to reintroduce rhinos back into the National Parks in Uganda through a breeding and release program. There are currently a total of six males and nine females in the Ziwa Sanctuary. There are a multitude of activities to do at the sanctuary including, rhino trekking, shoebill watching, birding watching and walking, and taking nature walks. There are also volunteer opportunities.

mission statement

The main mission of the sanctuary is to be able to apply effective conservation principles to create a safe environment where rhinos can be conserved for future generations. Additionally, through conservation education and community upliftment programs, gain support for rhino conservation and reintroduction programs within the community and Uganda as a whole.
